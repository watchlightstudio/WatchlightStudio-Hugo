## Overview

This month was largely about refining the Nucleate app and making significant strides in animation development. I spent a lot of time focusing on user interface improvements, particularly around dark mode and system-level GUIs. I faced some challenges, particularly with bugs and workflow inefficiencies that I need to address moving forward.

## Highlights & Progress

### Tools & Workflow
- Created seven out of ten animations using Blender, each taking between 5-8 hours. I experimented with geometry nodes, but the NLA Editor presented some challenges during the animation workflows.
- Switched from Tinker to PySide6 for UI panels, which I believe will streamline future development.
### Systems & Features
- Focused on refining the Nucleate app's user interface, especially the Labs section and dark mode conversion.
- Completed integrations with OpenAI, Notion, and Obsidian, with the Obsidian integration feeling particularly clean.
- Developed a panel for setting directories and redoing summaries in the Nucleate app, which should enhance user experience.
- Refactored system-level GUIs and updated pages like About, Calculator, and Licensing in the Nucleate app.
### Bugs & Issues
- Encountered a bug in the Nucleate app where weekly and monthly summaries weren't generating as expected, which needs further investigation.
- Noticed degradation in the quality of primary summaries due to the auto-note feature, raising questions about how to address this issue.

## Reflections

This month was a mixed bag. On one hand, I made good progress with the Nucleate app and the animations, but I also hit some snags that made me rethink my approach. The switch to PySide6 feels like a step in the right direction, but debugging issues related to Qt has been frustrating. I was surprised by how much time I spent on UI refinements, but I think they were necessary for improving user experience. The degradation in summary quality is a concern that I need to tackle, and I’m still figuring out the best way to market a freemium model.

## Next Steps

- Address the degradation in primary summaries due to the auto-note feature in the Nucleate app.
- Continue refining the animation workflows in Blender, focusing on the NLA Editor.
- Finalize the remaining hidden GUIs and color schemes in the Nucleate app for better intuitiveness.
- Develop specific marketing strategies for the freemium model to effectively reach potential users.
