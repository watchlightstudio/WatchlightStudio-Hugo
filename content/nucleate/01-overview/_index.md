## Meet Nucleate
Nucleate is a local-first, AI-powered note engine designed to turn spoken thoughts into structured, actionable notes — automatically.

This section covers the _why_ behind Nucleate: where it came from, what problem it solves, and how it’s different from cloud-first transcription tools. If you want the big picture before diving into features or setup, start here.
